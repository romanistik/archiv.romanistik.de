Associate Tutor in Spanish Translation

  • Ort: Surrey
  • Disziplinen: Sprachpraxis
  • Sprachen: Spanisch
  • Frist: 03.09.12

Associate Tutor in Spanish Translation

University of Surrey -School of English and Languages

 

Ref: 8950

 

Salary: £40 per hour plus 12.07% holiday pay

 

The School of English and Languages brings together the academic disciplines of English literature, creative writing, modern languages, linguistics, intercultural communication and translation studies. As a School we are interested in texts and languages in a global context. Our focus is on understanding how texts and languages work across national boundaries, how they function, and how they interact. Within the School we also offer the flagship Global Graduate Award, which gives all students in the University the opportunity to study a language, no matter what their degree scheme.

 

The School of English and Languages is looking for a part-time Associate Tutor to teach Spanish-English translation on its undergraduate programme (levels 2 and 3). The role consists of approximately 5 hours teaching per week with additional time for marking and assessment during Semester 1 (October - January).

 

Applicants should be native speakers of English and have a relevant degree/or equivalent along with teaching experience. Professional translation experience would be advantageous.

 

Please apply online at www.surrey.ac.uk/jobs. For any application queries, please contact Léonie Burton via email at leonie.burton@surrey.ac.uk or by telephone on 01483 689955 quoting reference number 8950.

 

For informal enquiries please email Margaret Rogers (m.rogers@surrey.ac.uk) or phone on 01483 682832.

 

Closing date for applications: 3rd September 2012

 

Interview date: w/c 10th September 2012

Von:  Margaret Rogers, 28.08.2012
