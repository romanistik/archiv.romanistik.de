Visiting Lecturer in Portuguese (hourly-paid)

www.regents.ac.uk

  • Frist: 12.11.12

Visiting Lecturer in Portuguese (hourly-paid)

Regent's College -Department of Languages and Cross-Cultural Studies

 

Salary: £44.00 per hour (£50.41 inclusive of holiday pay)

 

RC12/124/BAM

 

Faculty of Business and Management

 

We are looking for a Visiting Lecturer to deliver modules in Portuguese on the programmes within Regent's College. The postholder will be teaching mainly on the undergraduate BA in International Business within the Faculty of Business and Management, and s/he may also work on the evening classes programme, so the ability to teach in the evening (from 6:30 to 8:30) is desirable. The study of at least one foreign language, as well as at least one semester of study at a university in a country where that language is spoken, are integral parts of this 3 ½ year degree. Teaching may be at any level, from beginner to advanced, to students who are being prepared for their study period abroad at one of our partner universities in Portugal or Brazil, as well as to students who have already returned from their year abroad.

 

The postholder must hold a postgraduate degree in languages/applied linguistics or a business-related subject, be able to teach Portuguese from beginner's to advanced levels, and should possess a qualification in teaching Portuguese as a foreign language. Good interpersonal and communication skills are essential, as well as demonstrable experience in using IT to support language learning. Experience of teaching Portuguese for business is desirable.

 

To apply for this position, please send a letter of application detailing how you meet the requirements of the person specification (which is available online), together with your CV, to the Human Resources Department, Regent's College, Inner Circle, London, NW1 4NS. Applications by email are accepted.

 

We are an equal opportunities employer and are happy to consider applications from all individuals who meet the person specification.

 

Note: Please notify the College of any visa issues that might impact on your employment.

 

E-mail: hr@regents.ac.uk Web: www.regents.ac.uk Telephone +44 (0)20 7487 7739

 

Closing date: Monday 12th November, 2012 (5pm)

 

Interviews proposed: 29th November, 2012
