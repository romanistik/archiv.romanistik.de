Cultural Encounters - Commodity Chains - Labor Migration: World Regions - Transnationalities - Transidentities - Hybridities - Diasporisation

http://www.uni-leipzig.de/~iafsl/
  • Ort: Leipzig
  • Beginn: 27.10.10
  • Ende: 29.10.10

Section II, IAFSL/FFSL: Transnationalities – Transidentities – Hybridities - Diasporisation

 

The conference will debate the cultural processes of migration, border crossing, diasporization and a new understanding of concepts like nation or identies in the context of globalization.

 

Program:
