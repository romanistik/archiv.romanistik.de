Lecturer in Spanish

www.port.ac.uk/vacancies

  • Disziplinen: Sprachpraxis

  • Frist: 01.07.11

Lecturer in Spanish - 0.5 fte

University of Portsmouth - School of Languages and Area Studies, Faculty of Humanities and Social Sciences

 

Fixed term contract for 2 years

Salary: £15,898 - £17,373

Reference: 10002399

Closing date: 1st July 2011

 

The School wishes to appoint a 0.5 fte fixed term Lecturer to contribute to the teaching of a range of specialist and options units in Spanish, both at undergraduate degree level and on the Institution-Wide Languages Programme (IWLP).

 

You will be expected to be native or near-native speakers of Spanish and have proven experience of teaching Spanish to adult learners, preferably at University level. Experience of teaching Hispanic Area Studies is desirable. You should have a relevant first degree and preferably be a qualified language teacher. A relevant MA or PhD is desirable; the candidate will ideally be research and/or knowledge transfer-active, and will be expected to contribute to the School and/or CEISR's profile in these areas.

 

For further information or details of these positions, contact John Naysmith (Head of School), john.naysmith@port.ac.uk, 02392 846050 or Bob Gould (Divisional Coordinator, European & International Studies), robert.gould@port.ac.uk, 02392 846170.

 

Applications must be received before 12 noon on Friday 1st July 2011

 

To find out more about the University of Portsmouth and this role, visit www.port.ac.uk/vacancies and apply on-line. Alternatively telephone 023 9284 3421. Please quote the reference number on all communications.

 

All applications for this position will be processed and conducted in compliance with UK legislation relevant at that time.

Von:  John Naysmith
