30.05.2011

"Ordo" 8. Jahrestagung der International Medieval Society

  • Ort: Paris
  • Beginn: 30.06.11
  • Ende: 02.07.11
  • Disziplinen: Literaturwissenschaft, Sprachwissenschaft, Medien-/Kulturwissenschaft, Weitere Teilbereiche
  • Sprachen: Französisch

International Medieval Society / Société Internationale des Médiévistes

30.06.2011-02.07.2011, Paris, Centre Malher, 9 rue Malher, F-75004 Paris

 

In the Middle Ages the Latin term, ordo, designated all kind of concepts for classifying realities or ideas. Divine order as well as various human ordines created a certain stability which had to be reaffirmed time and again in order to retain its validity. Social, ideal, iconographic and other orders were established, maintained, and sometimes overturned, but even then the order had to be invoked.

 

What are these orders, these ways of thinking and ordering?

Particular emphasis will be placed on the question of knowing how

this medieval knowledge was ordered and classified.
